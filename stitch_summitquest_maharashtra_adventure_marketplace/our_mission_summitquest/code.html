The Sahyadri Spirit

We are more than guides. We are custodians of Maharashtra’s peaks, dedicated to making these ancient landscapes accessible, protected, and deeply understood.

Born from the Western Ghats

SummitQuest was founded by local mountaineers who saw a need for a higher standard of adventure. We noticed that while enthusiasm for trekking was growing, the pristine nature of our beloved Sahyadris was under threat, and safety standards varied wildly.

Our mission is twofold: to provide unparalleled, professionally curated experiences that challenge and inspire, while rigorously protecting the very environments we explore. We believe in 'Leave No Trace' not just as a slogan, but as an operational mandate.
