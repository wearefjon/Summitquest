The Challenge Awaits

Known as one of the most rugged and challenging treks in the Sahyadris, the ascent to Harishchandragad via the infamous Nalichi Vaat (Gorge Path) is a rite of passage for serious mountaineers. This near-vertical rock channel tests endurance, technique, and mental fortitude.

Our expertly curated expedition ensures safety without compromising the raw thrill of the climb. Guided by certified mountaineering professionals, you will navigate massive boulders, steep scree, and thrilling rock patches before emerging onto the vast plateau and witnessing the awe-inspiring Konkan Kada.

Expedition Itinerary

Day 1: The Ascent

Conquering the Gorge

The trek begins early from Belpada village. The initial walk through the forest quickly gives way to the base of the gorge. The next 6-7 hours are a strenuous climb over massive rocks and tricky natural rock steps. Ropes are deployed by our guides at crucial, exposed sections.

restaurant

Meals Included

Packed Lunch mid-climb, Hot Dinner at summit camp.

Day 2: Exploration & Descent

Konkan Kada & Kedareshwar

Wake up to a spectacular sunrise over the Sahyadris. Post breakfast, we visit the ancient Kedareshwar Cave and the majestic Konkan Kada cliff face. The descent is via the comparatively simpler Tolar Khind route, offering completely different views and a relaxing wind-down to the expedition.

Strict Safety Protocols

Nalichi Vaat is an extreme grade trek. We reserve the right to deny participation if fitness requirements are not met or inadequate footwear is observed. A strict 1:4 Guide-to-Trekker ratio is maintained. Ropes will be fixed at all three major rock patches.
