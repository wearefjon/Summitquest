Malshej Ghat

A rugged paradise of cascading waterfalls, ancient forts, and vibrant birdlife, perched high in the Western Ghats.

Overview

Nestled in the lofty rugged hills of the Western Ghats, Malshej Ghat is a renowned mountain pass in Maharashtra. Particularly spectacular during the monsoon months, the region transforms into a vibrant canvas of deep greens, adorned with countless waterfalls and enveloped in mystical mist. It's a haven for trekkers, nature lovers, and bird watchers, most famously known for the pink flamingos that visit during late summer.
